Your daily dosage of math,physics and chemistry!

What is it for?

A project which contains one theorem/principle/conjecture/concept (preferably for each day). It consists of the following subjects:-

  1. Maths
  2. Physics
  3. Chemistry

It starts with a simple proof on New Year's Day (If you start reading on 1st of Jan) and cycles through 3 days for every subject. We also colour code our boxes to represent the different subjects the things cover

Subject Color
Math Yellow
Physics Blue
Chemistry Red
